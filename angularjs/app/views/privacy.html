Methods of Collection:

RC uses many standard technologies to help us collect information including:

Cookies: We may automatically collect information using cookies, which are small data files stored on your hard drive by our (and many other) web sites. Cookies do not identify users, but commonly identify the user’s computer. We use cookies to save information regarding your use of the site and to help things run smoothly for example, by allowing you to ‘add an item’ to your shopping cart. We use also use cookies to keep your login information, save language and theme preferences and similar settings to make your browsing experience better. You can change the settings in your browser to disallow the use of cookies, however it may impair the functionality of our Services making them unavailable to you for use.

Other tracking technologies: We also store information on your computer that is directly related to the Services you use such as receipt data. This information is directly associated with your account and cannot be used by anyone else for any reason.
