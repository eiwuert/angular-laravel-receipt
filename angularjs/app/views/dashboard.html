Home Currency

{{responseMessage}}
Your setting has been updated successfully.
This is the currency that you use in the country in which you live or the currency you use for reporting purposes. For example, if you live in United States, you would choose USD. Once selected, this setting cannot be changed.
Home Currency*
Converting Foreign Currency Receipts:

For receipts or invoices in a foreign currency, you can use our powerful real-time Forex conversion tool on our Receipt Detail screen to instantaneously convert any of 165 global currencies to your home currency. You can even perform backdated conversions at their prevailing rates as far back as 1999.

Remember all foreign currency amounts have to be converted to the home currency. It is therefore very important for you to check if the Home Currency you have selected is correct for your account.
