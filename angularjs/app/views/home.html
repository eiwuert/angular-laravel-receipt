TravelExpense

Use our TravelExpense app to quickly and easily create and submit expense reports. Each report is neatly organized by trips; you can add as many trips to a single report as you want or need. When you’re ready to submit your report, click on the submit button and have it delivered instantly to your approver. You can also print and download your reports, which are complete with size-optimized copies of your receipt images cross- referenced to the expense items on your reports.

Our super-efficient Optical Character Recognition (OCR) engine extracts texts from receipts for automated data entry where possible, but you can also edit, drag and drop anything from the extracted text to the digital receipt. This saves you time on data entry so you can focus on your trip rather than spending hours creating your travel report. Our applications are available on the web, mobile and tablet devices so you can create and submit your expense reports on the go.

How it works: See what happens behind the scenes

ReceiptClub Image Processing Engine (RIPE)

Transforms a raw picture of receipt to a refined image.

RIPE, armed with our proprietary imaging algorithms, chops, crops, deskews, rotates, flips, binarizes, resizes and transforms it to a crisp, clean, zoomable, movable, printable image. The refined receipt image is then sent to ROCR.

ReceiptClub Optical Character Recognition (ROCR)

Extracts text from the refined receipt image

ROCR extracts readable text from the receipt image with an amazing degree of accuracy. Not only that, but it creates a formatted invisible layer of OCR right on top of the text on the receipt image such that you can literally highlight drag and drop any text from the receipt image to your digital receipt; true, not science fiction.

It also provides three different views of the receipt and OCR layer; plain image of receipt (OCR layer is invisible); click on “Highlight OCR” button, it highlights all OCR text in blue laid precisely over each letter in the image; click on “Show OCR” button, and system will display the OCR text in white on black background – like night vision. These options give you full clarity and access to the extracted data from your receipt.

Next, ROCR sends this composite package to RADE.

ReceiptClub Automated Data Entry (RADE)

Creates an auto-filled digital receipt

RADE parses the extracted machine-readable receipt text from ROCR and auto-fills a digital version of the receipt or invoice, including, in many cases, line item data. RADE can also categorize this information using artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms. Say goodbye to manual data entry!

Validate

In most cases you can validate your receipt or invoice data with the click of a button and you’re done. However, if there are any validation errors, you can easily edit and correct the digital receipt with our cool drag & drop feature.

For receipts or invoices in a foreign currency, you can use our powerful, real-time Forex conversion tool to instantaneously convert any of 165 global currencies to your home currency. You can even perform backdated conversions at their prevailing rates as far back as 1999.
